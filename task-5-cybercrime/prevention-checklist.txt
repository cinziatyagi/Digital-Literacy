Stay Safe Online Checklist:

- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
- Always check the URL of websites before entering details
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or unknown emails
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Use secure and trusted Wi-Fi networks only
- Regularly update your apps and devices
- Verify messages claiming to be from banks or officials

UPI Safety Tips:
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Never share UPI PIN with anyone

Report Cybercrime:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
